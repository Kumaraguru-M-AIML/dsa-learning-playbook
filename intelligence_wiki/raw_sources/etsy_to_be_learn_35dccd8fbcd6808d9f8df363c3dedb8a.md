- “Make one viral product”
- “Look creative”
- “Get lucky”

But:

```
Own a repeatable demand-capture system.
```

Meaning:

- You know how to:
    - identify demand
    - create listings
    - rank products
    - convert buyers
    - scale winners

Once you learn that, you can:

- launch multiple stores
- move to other platforms
- build brands outside Etsy
