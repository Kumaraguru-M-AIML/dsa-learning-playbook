

## Qube Cinema -– Medium Level Quants Questions (40)
## Number System
- Find the remainder when 7^103 is divided by 8.
- How many numbers between 1 and 500 are divisible by 6 but not by 9?
- The HCF of two numbers is 12 and their LCM is 720. If one number is 120, find the other.
- Find the unit digit of 3^57.
Profit and Loss
- A shopkeeper marks an item at 25% above cost price and gives a 10% discount. Find his
profit percentage.
- An article is sold for ₹840 at a loss of 16%. Find the cost price.
- A trader gains 20% by selling a product for ₹960. What is the cost price?
Simple Interest (SI) and Compound Interest (CI)
- Find the simple interest on ₹12,000 at 8% per annum for 3 years.
- Find the compound interest on ₹10,000 at 10% per annum for 2 years (compounded
annually).
- The difference between CI and SI on a sum for 2 years at 5% is ₹50. Find the principal.
Time, Speed and Distance
- A car travels 180 km in 3 hours. What is its average speed?
- Two cities are 420 km apart. A train travels at 60 km/h. How long will it take to reach?
- A person walks at 5 km/h and is 2 km behind a bus moving at 25 km/h. How long will it
take the bus to be 7 km ahead?
## Trains
- A train 150 m long passes a pole in 9 seconds. Find its speed in km/h.
- A train 200 m long crosses a platform 300 m long in 25 seconds. Find the speed of the
train.
- Two trains of lengths 120 m and 180 m move in opposite directions at 54 km/h and 36
km/h. Time taken to cross each other?

Mixture and Alligation
- A 30 L mixture of milk and water is in the ratio 4:1. How much water must be added to
make the ratio 3:2?
- Rice worth ₹40/kg is mixed with rice worth ₹60/kg in the ratio 3:2. Find the mixture
price per kg.
- In what ratio should two solutions of 20% and 50% acid be mixed to get a 32% acid
solution?
Boats and Streams
- A boat travels 30 km downstream in 2 hours and returns upstream in 3 hours. Find the
speed of the boat in still water.
- Speed of a boat in still water is 12 km/h and stream speed is 3 km/h. Find downstream
and upstream speeds.
- A boat covers 24 km downstream in the same time it covers 16 km upstream. If stream
speed is 2 km/h, find boat speed in still water.

## Blood Relationships
- Pointing to a man, Ravi said, 'His mother is the only daughter of my mother.' How is the
man related to Ravi?
- A is the brother of B. C is the mother of A. D is the father of C. How is D related to B?
- P is Q's sister. R is Q's mother. S is R's father. How is S related to P?

nCr (Combinations)
- How many ways can 3 students be selected from a group of 8 students?
- Find the value of 10C3.
- In how many ways can a committee of 4 be formed from 7 men and 5 women if exactly 2
women must be included?
nPr (Permutations)
- Find the value of 6P3.
- How many 4-digit numbers can be formed using digits 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 without repetition?
- In how many ways can 5 people sit in a row if two particular people must sit together?

## Series

- Find the next number: 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?
- Find the missing term: 5, 11, 23, 47, ?, 191
- Find the next term: 1, 4, 9, 16, 25, ?
AP (Arithmetic Progression)

- Find the 15th term of the AP: 3, 7, 11, 15, ...

- Find the sum of first 20 terms of AP: 5, 8, 11, ...

- If the 7th term of an AP is 31 and common difference is 4, find the first term.

GP (Geometric Progression)

- Find the 6th term of GP: 2, 4, 8, 16, ...

- Find the sum of first 5 terms of GP: 3, 6, 12, ...

- If the first term of a GP is 5 and common ratio is 3, find the 4th term.
