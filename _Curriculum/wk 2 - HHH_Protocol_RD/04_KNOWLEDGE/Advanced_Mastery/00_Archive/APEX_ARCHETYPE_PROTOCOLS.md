# APEX ARCHETYPE PHYSICAL PROTOCOLS
### How Each Archetype Trains — Real-World Application Blueprints

## ARCHETYPE I: WILD ASSASSIN (Predatory Lethal Efficiency)

```
MODEL: Big cats (leopard, panther, tiger) — NOT endurance herd animals, NOT gorillas
CORE RULE: Built to strike first, move silently, recover instantly, and disappear.
Every stat exists to serve LETHALITY + EVASION.
```

| Stat | Priority | Training Emphasis | Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Strength** | High force, ZERO bulk | Weighted calisthenics, isometrics (mid + end range), low-rep compounds (no grinding) | Assassin strength = control, not numbers |
| **Speed & Power** | PRIMARY KILL STAT | Short max sprints, low-volume plyometrics, ballistic throws | Fights, escapes, dominance decided immediately |
| **Endurance** | Silent engine | Zone 2 only, minimal VO₂ max, no long suffering | Supports speed, never replaces it |
| **Mobility** | Predatory range | Loaded mobility, animal flow, gymnastic control | Loose = dead. Controlled = lethal. |
| **Stability** | NON-NEGOTIABLE | Rings, single-leg patterns, chaos drills | Precision collapses without balance |
| **Body Comp** | Weaponized frame | Lean, compact, dense. No bulk cycles, no extreme cuts. | Every useless kg slows the strike |
| **Recovery** | Predator survival | High sleep consistency, fast NS downshift, early deloads | The predator that can't recover becomes prey |

**Trade-Off Logic:**

| Conflict | Assassin Choice |
|---|---|
| Strength vs Size | Strength wins |
| Speed vs Endurance | Speed wins |
| Flexibility vs Stability | Stability first |
| Peak vs Longevity | Longevity |

| **Body Comp** | Deceptive average | No bulking cycles, maintain athletic BF%, lean compact frame | Appear unremarkable — underestimated people move freely |
| **Recovery** | Hidden stat priority | Ruthless sleep consistency, early deloads, breathwork for parasympathetic | Ayanokoji never looks strained |
