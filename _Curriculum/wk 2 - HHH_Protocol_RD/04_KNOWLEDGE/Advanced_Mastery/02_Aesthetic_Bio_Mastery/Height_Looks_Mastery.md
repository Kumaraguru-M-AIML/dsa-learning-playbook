# PART A: HEIGHT MAXIMIZATION

## 1. THE SCIENCE OF HEIGHT GROWTH

### 1.1 What Determines Height?
1.  **Genetics (60-80%):** Your DNA sets the *potential* range.
2.  **Hormones (Growth Hormone, IGF-1):** The *fuel* for bone growth.
3.  **Nutrition (Protein, Calcium, Zinc, Vit D):** The *raw materials*.
4.  **Exercise (Stretching, Hanging, HIIT):** The *signal* to grow.
5.  **Sleep (7.5-9 hrs):** When Growth Hormone is *released*.
6.  **Posture:** Can add 1-3 cm of *apparent* height instantly.

### 1.2 Factors That STUNT Growth (Mistakes to Avoid)
- **Poor Nutrition:** Lack of protein, calcium, zinc.
- **Sleep Deprivation:** Crushing GH production.
- **Bad Posture (Spinal Compression):** Hunching, slouching.
- **Chronic Stress (Cortisol):** Blocks GH receptors.
- **Sedentary Lifestyle:** No signal to grow.
- **Early Heavy Lifting (Controversy):** Some evidence suggests excessive spinal loading before 18 can compress cartilage. Focus on bodyweight first.

## 2. THE TITAN METHOD (Synthesis)

### 2.1 The "Titan Stretches"
1.  **Cobra Stretch:** Lie on stomach, push torso up with arms. Hold 15-30s. Repeat 3x. Targets spine/chest.
2.  **Hanging Stretch:** Dead hang from bar, 30-60s. Repeat 3x. Decompresses spine.
3.  **Pelvic Shift:** Lie on back, feet flat, lift hips. Hold 20s. Opens lower spine.
4.  **Super Cobra:** From all fours, transition to cobra. Repeat.

### 2.2 The "Titan Diet"
- **Protein (1.6-2g/kg BW):** Eggs, fish, chicken, legumes.
- **Calcium (1000-1300mg/day):** Dairy, leafy greens.
- **Vitamin D (600-1000 IU/day):** Sunlight, fatty fish.
- **Zinc (8-11mg/day):** Oysters, beef, pumpkin seeds.
- **Hydration (3-4L/day):** Dehydration compresses cartilage.

### 2.3 The "Titan Supplements"
- **Vitamin D3:** The critical hormone-vitamin.
- **Calcium + Magnesium:** Bone building blocks.
- **Zinc:** GH production support.
- **Arginine (Optional, Advanced):** May stimulate GH release.

## 2. MEWING (Synthesis from "The Mewing Bible")

### 2.1 What is Mewing?
Proper tongue posture: Entire tongue pressed flat against the roof of the mouth (palate), with lips sealed and teeth lightly touching.

### 2.2 Benefits (Long-Term)
- Forward maxilla growth (better face projection).
- Defined jawline.
- Wider palate.
- Better breathing.

### 2.3 Protocol
1.  **Awareness:** Check tongue posture every hour.
2.  **Lip Seal:** Mouth closed, lips together.
3.  **Swallow Correctly:** Use tongue, not cheeks.
4.  **Chew Hard Foods:** Strengthens masseter muscles.
