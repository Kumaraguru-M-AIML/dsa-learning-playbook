## THE COMPOUNDING KILLERS — WHAT TO PROTECT AGAINST

| Killer | Mechanism of Destruction | Prevention Protocol |
|---|---|---|
| **Injury** | Erases 6-18 months of compounding. Resets trajectory backward. | Structural durability work from Year 1. Zero tolerance for training through structural damage. |
| **Stagnation** | Doesn't just stop compounding — fails to build the adaptation infrastructure that makes future compounding possible. | Program redesign the MOMENT progress stalls for >3 months. |
| **Overtraining** | Creates NEGATIVE compounding — fatigue masks fitness, hormonal disruption worsens, motivation degrades, injury risk spikes. | HRV monitoring + total allostatic load management. |
| **Specificity Loss** | One layer falls far behind and limits ALL others — compounding slows across the entire system even as other layers improve. | Full 9-layer assessment every 6 months. |
