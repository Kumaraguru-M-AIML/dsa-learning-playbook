## ARCHETYPE 1 — SUNG JIN-WOO
### The Infinite Scaling System

```
Core Reality: He wins because his GROWTH ENGINE never turns off.
Not talent. SYSTEM.
```

**Growth Engine Structure:**
```
Input:    Training stress (progressive overload)
Process:  High-quality training protocols + extraction
Feedback: PRs, speed, fatigue signals, output quality
Compound: Strength → more load capacity → more strength
Recovery: Sleep, injury prevention, mental reset

If any one part breaks → growth stalls.
All parts running → growth is inevitable.
```

**7-Stat Real-World Standard:**

| Stat | Priority | Standard |
|---|---|---|
| Strength | Primary | Heavy compounds, neural-first, no cap |
| Speed & Power | High — scales with strength | Sprint + plyometrics |
| Endurance | Medium — sustains engine | Zone 2 base, enough VO₂ |
| Mobility | Enabler | Loaded mobility, gymnastics control |
| Stability | Efficiency stat | Rings, unilateral, anti-rotation |
| Body Composition | Cycled hypertrophy → consolidation | Lean, cyclical |
| Recovery | THE MACHINE | Sleep + deloads + parasympathetic |

**Key Law:**
> Sung Jin-Woo doesn't train harder. His system compounds while others plateau.

**Real-World Translation:**
1. One core skill (your daggers = your primary compound movement)
2. One scaling system (weekly progressive overload)
3. External multipliers (tools, tracking, environment)
4. Relentless recovery (sleep 8h, deload every 4-6 weeks)
5. Information advantage (know your limits before you reach them)
