Stretching and Warm-Up

1. Wrist Stretches

Before engaging in any exercise, it's crucial to warm up and
stretch properly to prevent injuries. Calisthenics exercises heavily
rely on your wrists and shoulders, so it's important to prepare
these areas adequately.

For wrists, perform a series of wrist poses with different
hand positions. Ease into these stretches with short,
one-second holds for 10 to 15 reps in each position. Aim to
achieve at least 90 degrees of wrist extension comfortably
over time, as it plays a signiﬁcant role in many calisthenics
exercises.

twice a week.
