## 🛠️ THE 2-MONTH "HOME BASE" PROTOCOL
*Strategic focus: Building infrastructure before gym loading.*

### The 4 Daily Non-Negotiables
1. **Walking/Cycling**: 8,000–10,000 steps OR 20–30m cycling (Nasal breathing only).
2. **Mobility Routine**: 10 mins (Deep Squat, Hip Flexor, Thoracic Rotation, Shoulder Circles).
3. **Sleep**: 7.5–9 hours (The primary recovery engine).
4. **Nutrition**: 120–130g Protein, small caloric deficit, zero ultra-processed food.

### The 3 Weekly Training Sessions (Full System Integration)
*Rule: 2-1-2 Tempo | 3s Eccentric (Lowering) | Never train to failure.*

#### **Session A: Neural Awakening**
- **Goblet Squat**: 4 sets × 5 reps (6kg)
- **Single-Leg Glute Bridge**: 3 sets × 5 reps/side
- **Push-ups (Standard/Parallette)**: 4 sets × 4–6 reps
- **Dumbbell Row**: 4 sets × 5 reps/side
- **Farmer Carry**: 3 sets × 30s
- **Plank + L-Sit Accumulation**: 3 sets / 20s

#### **Session B: Control & Stability**
- **Split Squat**: 3 sets × 5 reps/side
- **Romanian Deadlift**: 4 sets × 5 reps (6kg)
- **Pike Push-up**: 3 sets × 4–5 reps
- **Inverted Row (Table)**: 3 sets × 5–6 reps
- **Suitcase Carry**: 3 sets × 20–25s/side
- **Side Plank + Hollow Body**: 3 sets / 20s

#### **Session C: Power Primer (Month 2 Only)**
- **Jump Squats**: 4 sets × 3 reps (Explosive, land soft)
- **Explosive Push-up**: 3 sets × 3 reps
- **Jump Rope**: 10 mins intervals (Nasal breathing)
