# HHH PHASE 1: THE ZERO-COST FORGE (0-6 Months)
*Target: The Unforged Strategist (20 yrs)*

## 🛡️ MISSION OBJECTIVE
Establish the biological and neural foundation. Transition the system from "Comfort/Chaos" to "Structured Pressure."

---

## ⚔️ DIMENSION 3: PHYSICAL CONSTRUCTION (The Vessel)
*Frequency: 6 Days / Week | Cost: $0*

### 1. The Morning Pulse (Daily)
*Immediately upon waking.*
- **100 Burpees** (For time). Signals the heart and lungs that the day has begun.
- **Sunlight Exposure** (10 mins). Pins the circadian rhythm.

### 2. The Frame-Work (Mon/Wed/Fri)
*Focus: Structural integrity and the V-Taper.*
- **Pull:** 5 sets of Max Pullups (Find a tree branch or playground).
- **Push:** 5 sets of Max Pseudo-Planche Pushups (Lean forward to put weight on wrists/shoulders).
- **Core:** 3 sets of Hollow Body Holds (1 min).
- **Lower:** 5 sets of 20 Bulgarian Split Squats (Use a chair/ledge).

### 3. The Isometric Siege (Tue/Thu/Sat)
*Focus: Tendon hardening and neural recruitment.*
- **Hanging:** 3x1 min Dead Hang (Spinal decompression + grip).
- **Wall Sit:** 3x2 mins (Lower body endurance).
- **Plank:** 3xMax (Internal pressure).

---

## 🧠 DIMENSION 4: NEURO-DOMINANCE (The Command)
*Objective: Dopamine Resensitization.*

### 1. The Information Blackout (Month 1)
- **Zero Social Media Scrolling.**
- **Zero Pornography.**
- **Zero Processed Sugar.**
- *Why:* To lower the dopamine baseline so that "The Grind" becomes the primary reward.

### 2. The Deep Work Block (Daily)
- **2 Hours of Focused Learning** (Morning). 
- **Rule:** Phone in a different room. No music (or Brown Noise only).
- **Target:** Master one high-leverage skill from the `Extracted_Mastery` folder.

### 3. The Stoic Filter
- **Cold Showers (Daily).** Voluntary discomfort to decouple the "Pain Signal" from the "Stop Action."
- **Internal Audit.** Record every time you felt "fear" or "laziness" and choose the opposite action once per day.

---

## 🔋 DIMENSION 8: FUEL & RECOVERY
- **Sleep Architecture:** Fixed wake time (e.g., 5 AM). No screens 1 hour before bed.
- **The "Bison" Diet:** High-density, cheap staples (Eggs, Rice/Potatoes, Seasonal Veg, Water).
- **Hydration:** 4L Water per day.

---

## 📈 MONTHLY MILESTONES
- **Month 2**: Bodyweight should start shifting (Leanness).
- **Month 4**: Cognitive fog should be 90% cleared.
- **Month 6**: The "Frame" is established. You are ready for Phase 2 (Hypertrophy).
