## 🏗️ THE CORE PHILOSOPHY: PROGRESSIVE BIOLOGICAL LOADING
You don't just "work out." You **engineer an adaptation**. To go from "nothing" to "athletic baseline," you must scaffold your progress so that your **nervous system, tendons, and hormones** keep up with your muscles.

### 🌓 STAGE 0: STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY (The Reset)
**Objective:** Fix the "Collapsed Frame" (ATP, Kyphosis, Compressed Spine).
- **Primary Source:** *6FT6 Method*, *Convict Conditioning Lvl 1*.
- **Keywords:** `Decompression`, `Proprioception`, `Joint-Mobility`.
- **The Scaffold:** 
    - You cannot lift heavy weights if your spine is compressed or your pelvis is tilted. 
    - **Actions:** 3min Dead Hangs, Wall Angels, Glute Bridges.
    - **Benchmark:** Hold a perfect plank for 60s without lower back pain.

### 🌕 STAGE 1: NEUROMUSCULAR MASTERY (The Foundation)
**Objective:** Teach the brain how to recruit muscle fibers.
- **Primary Source:** *Convict Conditioning*, *Building the Gymnastic Body*.
- **Keywords:** `CNS-Conditioning`, `Time-Under-Tension`, `Pillar-Strength`.
- **The Scaffold:** 
    - Use slow, controlled bodyweight movements to "awaken" the motor units.
    - **Actions:** High-volume pushups (wall/incline), Bodyweight squats (Assisted if needed).
    - **Benchmark:** 20 Clean Pushups, 30 Full Squats.

### 🌔 STAGE 2: HYPERTROPHY & ANABOLIC LOADING (The Engine)
**Objective:** Pack on the biological armor (Functional Muscle).
- **Primary Source:** *Jeff Nippard Fundamentals*, *Testosterone Maxxing*.
- **Keywords:** `Progressive-Overload`, `Volume`, `Hormonal-Substrate`.
- **The Scaffold:** 
    - Now that the structure is solid, we add load. We use compound lifts to signal a massive Hormonal response.
    - **Actions:** Weighted Pullups/Pushups, Dumbbell Squats, Barbell Rows.
    - **Benchmark:** Pullup (1 Rep), Pushup (40 Reps), Squat (50 Reps).

### ☀️ STAGE 3: EXPLOSIVE CAPABILITY (The Athlete)
**Objective:** Convert strength into power and speed.
- **Primary Source:** *SuperFunctional Training*, *Sprinting Protocols*.
- **Keywords:** `Plyometrics`, `Kinetic-Chain`, `Metabolic-Engine`.
- **The Scaffold:** 
    - We take the muscle we build and make it "fast."
    - **Actions:** Hill Sprints, Jump Squats, Shadow Boxing.
    - **Benchmark:** 5KM Run (<25m), 10 Pullups, 100m Sprint (<14s).
