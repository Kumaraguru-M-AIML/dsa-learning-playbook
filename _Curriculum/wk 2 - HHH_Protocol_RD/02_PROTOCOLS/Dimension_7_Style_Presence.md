# 👔 DIMENSION 7: STYLE & PRESENCE MASTERY
*Engineering the Visual Avatar.*

## 1. WARDROBE ALGORITHMS
**Objective:** Communicate tailored dominance.
- **Fit > Brand:** A $20 tailored shirt beats a $200 baggy one.
- **The Uniform:** Black T-shirt (fitted arms), Dark Wash Jeans/Chinos, Chelsea Boots/Clean Sneakers.
- **Colors:** Navy, Black, Charcoal, White, Olive. No neon. No massive logos.

## 2. GROOMING STANDARDS
**Objective:** Impeccable hygiene details.
- **Hair:** Barbershop every 2-3 weeks. Don't let the neck fuzz grow.
- **Beard:** Lined up cheek/neck line. Stubborn stubble > Patchy beard.
- **Hands:** Nails clipped and clean. No one trusts a man with dirty nails.

## 3. OLFACTORY SIGNATURE
**Objective:** Scent memory.
- **Fragrance:** Apply to pulse points (neck, wrists).
- **Notes:** Wood, Leather, Tobacco (Masculine) > Citrus/Sport (Generic).
