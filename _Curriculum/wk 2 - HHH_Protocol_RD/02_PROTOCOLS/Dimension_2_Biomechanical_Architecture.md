# 🦾 DIMENSION 2: BIOMECHANICAL ARCHITECTURE
*The Structural Integrity of the Machine.*

## 1. WOLFF'S LAW (Bone Hardening)
- **Micro-fracture Loading**: High-impact movements (Sprinting, Jumping) paired with high mineral intake (Calcium/Magnesium/D3/K2) to increase bone density.
- **Micro-Skill**: Knuckle and shin conditioning (derived from Muay Thai/Karate fundamentals).

## 2. DECOMPRESSION & HEIGHT
- **Inversion Therapy**: Hanging (Pull-up bar) to decompress the spine and maximize reached height.
- **Posture Reset**: Correcting Anterior Pelvic Tilt (APT) to restore natural height and aesthetic alignment.

## 3. FLEXIBILITY & JOINT ARMOR
- **Tendon Hardening**: Isometric holds at extreme ranges of motion (e.g., Horse Stance).
- **Joint Lubrication**: Collagen + Vitamin C synergy + Slow controlled eccentric movements.
