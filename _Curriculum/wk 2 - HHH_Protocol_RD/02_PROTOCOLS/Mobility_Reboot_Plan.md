# 📈 MOBILITY REBOOT: 4-WEEK EXECUTION PLAN
### 「 Awakening the Stagnant System 」

> **"You have been offline for months. This plan is your 'Safe Mode' bootloader. We will slowly reintroduce range before we apply load."**

---

## 📅 WEEK 1: THE AWAKENING
*Goal: Break the sedentary seal and reset fascial tension.*

- **Frequency**: 7 Days / Week
- **Duration**: 10-15 Minutes
- **The Protocol**:
    1. **Nasal Breathing (3 min)**: Calm the nervous system before moving.
    2. **Cat-Cow (10 reps)**: Restore spinal fluid flow.
    3. **Wall Ankle Stretch (30s/side)**: Fix the walking foundation.
    4. **Deep Squat Hold (Accumulate 2 min)**: Use support (chair/doorframe) if needed.
    5. **Dead Hangs (30s total)**: Decompress the spine.

---

## 📅 WEEK 2: RANGE DISCOVERY
*Goal: Explore the edges of the joint capsules.*

- **Frequency**: 6 Days / Week (1 Rest Day)
- **Duration**: 20 Minutes
- **The Protocol**:
    1. **Everything from Week 1** (Shortened to 5 mins).
    2. **90/90 Hip Switches (10 reps)**: Begin hip rotation recovery.
    3. **Thoracic Windmills (10/side)**: Unlock the ribcage for breathing.
    4. **Tibialis Raises (15 reps)**: Strengthen the lower chain.
    5. **Scapular CARs (10 reps)**: Learn to move the shoulder blades independently.

---

## 📅 WEEK 3: ACTIVE STABILIZATION
*Goal: Owning the new ranges with isometric strength.*

- **Frequency**: 5 Days / Week
- **Duration**: 25 Minutes
- **The Protocol**:
    1. **Dynamic Warmup (5 min)**: (Cat-Cow, Circles).
    2. **Couch Stretch (1 min/side)**: The most critical move for desk-bound systems.
    3. **Frog Stretch with PNF (60s)**: Push knees into ground for 5s, then relax deeper.
    4. **Bird-Dog (10/side)**: Connect core to cross-body mobility.
    5. **Prone Y-W-T (10 reps)**: Activate the back to fix posture.

---

## 📅 WEEK 4: KINETIC INTEGRATION
*Goal: Linking joints into a single predator-movement system.*

- **Frequency**: 5 Days / Week
- **Duration**: 30 Minutes
- **The Protocol**:
    1. **The "World's Greatest Stretch" (5 reps/side)**: The core flow.
    2. **Jefferson Curls (Unloaded - 5 reps)**: Full posterior chain elongation.
    3. **Horse Stance Hold (30s)**: Build structural endurance.
    4. **Shoulder Dislocates (PVC/Band - 15 reps)**: Full shoulder arc.
    5. **Final Audit**: Record your new range limits in `JOURNAL.md`.

---
