### ⚠️ THE APEX RULES OF MOBILITY
1. **Never Force Pain**: Discomfort (stretching) is fine; sharp pain is a "Stop Signal."
2. **Breathe into the Range**: If you can't breathe (Nasal), you don't own the range.
3. **Consistency over Intensity**: 10 mins daily > 1 hour once a week.
