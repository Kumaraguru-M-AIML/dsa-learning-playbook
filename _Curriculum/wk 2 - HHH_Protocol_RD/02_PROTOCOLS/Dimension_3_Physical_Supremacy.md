# ⚔️ DIMENSION 3: PHYSICAL SUPREMACY MASTERY
*Construction of the Lethal & Aesthetic Physique.*

## 1. THE BIG SIX (Survival Strength)
*Derived from: Convict Conditioning. Focus on joint/tendon integrity.*
1.  **The Pushup**: Armor-plated pecs/triceps. Progress to One-Arm.
2.  **The Squat**: Elevator cable thighs. Progress to One-Leg (Pistol).
3.  **The Pullup**: Barn door back. Progress to One-Arm.
4.  **The Leg Raise**: Six-pack from hell. Progress to Hanging Toes-to-Bar.
5.  **The Bridge**: Combat-ready spine & neck.
6.  **The Handstand Pushup**: Shoulder dominance & stability.

## 2. THE LEVERAGE LOGIC
- **Conditioning**: High intensity Burpees/Sprints (8x200m) to trigger HGH.
- **Tendon Hardening**: Controlled negatives (3 seconds down) and isometric holds.
- **Micro-Skill**: Shadowboxing and Grappling defense fundamentals.
