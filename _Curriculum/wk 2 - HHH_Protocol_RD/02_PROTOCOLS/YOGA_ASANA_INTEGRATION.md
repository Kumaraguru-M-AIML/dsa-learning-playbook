Studies indicate up to a **35% improvement in flexibility** after just 8 weeks of consistent practice. Unlike basic stretching, Yoga combines:

## 🏗️ CORE ASANAS FOR APEX MOBILITY
These poses target the "Big 3" structural junctions: Hips, Shoulders, and Spine.

### 1. THE WARRIOR SERIES (Virabhadrasana)
- **Focus**: Dynamic hip extension, pelvic stability, and shoulder reach.
- **Apex Benefit**: Builds the "First-Step" explosiveness required for predators.

### 2. THE TREE POSE (Vrikshasana)
- **Focus**: Ankle stability, hip external rotation, and global balance.
- **Apex Benefit**: Enhances proprioception and neural control under fatigue.

### 3. THE COBRA / UP-DOG (Bhujangasana)
- **Focus**: Spinal extension and anterior chain opening.
- **Apex Benefit**: Reverses the "Sedentary Hunch" and restores lung capacity.
