# 1. TACTICAL: KGB ALPHA TEAM PROTOCOLS
*Source: KGB Alpha Team Training Manual*

### 1.1 The "Phantom" Movement (Silent Walking)
1. **The Lead:** Place the outer edge of the ball of the foot down first.
2. **The Roll:** Slowly roll the weight to the inside and then to the heel.
3. **The Center:** Keep knees slightly bent at all times to absorb sound and allow for instant spring.
4. **Surface Analysis:** In dry leaves or gravel, "dig" slightly to reach more stable/silent layers before applying full weight.

### 1.2 Neural Reset (The Banya Method)
1. **Heat:** 10-15 minutes of high-intensity heat (steam).
2. **Shock:** 30-60 seconds of ice-cold immersion or snow.
3. **Rest:** 10 minutes of horizontal rest.
4. **Purpose:** Force blood from core to extremities and back, flushing metabolic waste and "hardening" the autonomic nervous system.

# 2. STRUCTURAL: CONVICT CONDITIONING (CC)
*Source: Paul Wade*

### 2.1 The "Bodyweight 10" Progression
Every move must follow a 10-step progression. Do not skip.
- **Novice:** Steps 1-3 (e.g., Wall Pushups, Knee Squats).
- **Intermediate:** Steps 4-6 (e.g., Half Pushups, Full Squats).
- **Advanced:** Steps 7-9 (e.g., Uneven Pullups, Bridge Wall Walks).
- **Master:** Step 10 (e.g., One-Arm Pushup, One-Leg Squat).

### 2.2 The "2-1-2" Tempo
- **2 Seconds Down (Eccentric):** Build control and tendon strength.
- **1 Second Hold (Isometric):** Eliminate momentum.
- **2 Seconds Up (Concentric):** Focus on muscle fibers.
- **Why:** This tempo prevents injury and ensures that the *tendons* are being conditioned alongside the muscles.

*Source: Adam Sinicki*

# 4. MECHANICS: OVERCOMING GRAVITY / GYMNASTIC BODY
*Sources: Steven Low / Christopher Sommer*

### 4.1 Structural Integrity (Prehab)
1. **The Scapular Base:** Mastering scapular shrugs, protraction, and retraction is non-negotiable before attempting planches or front levers.
2. **Straight Arm Strength:** Conditioning the bicep tendons through static holds (Support Hold, RDL) to prevent distal bicep tears.
3. **Periodization:** Simple Linear Progression is for beginners; moving to "Light/Heavy" loading once hitting Step 6 on the CC scale.

### 4.2 The "Steady State" Protocol (Coach Sommer)
1. **The Accumulation Phase:** Performing a static hold at 50% max duration for 4-8 sets.
2. **The 8-12 Week Cycle:** Maintaining the SAME intensity for up to 3 months to allow connective tissue (fascia/tendons) to catch up to muscle strength.
3. **The "Non-Negotiable" Quality:** Form must be 100% perfect. A single degree of elbow bend invalidates the hold.
