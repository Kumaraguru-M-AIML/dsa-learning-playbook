# 6. THE EXECUTION SYSTEMS

## 6.1 Pomodoro Technique
- **Standard:** 25 min work / 5 min break.
- **Deep Work:** 50 min work / 10 min break.
- **Creative:** 90 min work / 20 min break (full ultradian cycle).

## 6.2 Habit Stacking
> **After [ANCHOR HABIT], I will [TINY BEHAVIOR], then I will [CELEBRATE].**

## 6.3 Goal Setting (Locke & Latham)
- **Specific + Challenging Goals → Higher Performance.**
- **Process Goals > Outcome Goals.** (Control actions, not results.)
- **Goal Gradient:** Motivation increases as you approach the finish line.

## 6.4 Micro-Actions (Kaizen)
- **The 2-Minute Rule:** Scale any habit down to <2 minutes.
- **The 1% Rule:** 1.01^365 = 37x improvement.
- **Never Miss Twice:** Missing once is an accident. Twice is a new habit.

## 6.5 Streaks & Commitment Devices
- **Loss Aversion:** Breaking a streak feels 2x worse than starting one.
- **Accountability Partner:** Social commitment increases adherence.
- **Hard Commitment:** Financial stakes (e.g., Stickk.com).

## 6.6 Progressive Overload
- **Rate:** 2-5% increase per week. >10% risks injury/burnout.
- **Methods:** Increase load, volume, complexity, or decrease rest.
- **Periodization:** Cycle high-intensity and recovery phases.
