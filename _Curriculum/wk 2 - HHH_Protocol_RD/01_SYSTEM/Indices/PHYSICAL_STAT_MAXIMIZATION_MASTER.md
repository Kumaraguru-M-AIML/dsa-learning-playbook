## THE 7-STAT APEX ARCHITECTURE

### STAT 1: STRENGTH — Force Production

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Maximum neural drive + muscle cross-section |
| **Training Style** | Heavy compounds (1-5 reps), Olympic lifts, weighted calisthenics, isometrics |
| **Key Adaptations** | CNS efficiency, bone density, tendon stiffness |
| **Ceiling Constraint** | Recovery + connective tissue tolerance |
| **Apex Benchmarks** | Pull-ups 20+, Weighted pull-up +50%BW, Back squat 2×BW, Deadlift 2.5×BW, Push-ups 60+ |

---

### STAT 2: SPEED & POWER — Rate of Force Development

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Explosiveness, acceleration, reactivity |
| **Training Style** | Max sprints (10-60m), plyometrics (depth jumps, bounds), ballistic throws, COD drills |
| **Key Adaptations** | Fast-twitch fiber recruitment, elastic energy reuse, neural timing precision |
| **Ceiling Constraint** | Tendon health, nervous system fatigue |
| **Apex Benchmarks** | 30m ≤ 4.2s, 100m 11-12s, Vertical jump 60-75cm, Broad jump 2.4-2.7m, Reaction time <200ms |

**RULE:** Speed is trained FRESH, never tired. Always before heavy lifting.

---

### STAT 3: ENDURANCE — Energy Systems

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Cardiac output + mitochondrial density |
| **Training Style** | Zone 2 aerobic base (engine), VO₂ max intervals (ceiling), lactate threshold work |
| **Key Adaptations** | Stroke volume, capillarization, metabolic efficiency |
| **Ceiling Constraint** | Muscle mass vs endurance interference |
| **Apex Benchmarks** | VO₂ max 55-65 ml/kg/min, RHR 45-55 bpm, 5km <22min, Zone-2 90+ min sustainable |

---

### STAT 4: MOBILITY & FLEXIBILITY — Usable Range

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Full ROM under ACTIVE control |
| **Training Style** | Active flexibility (loaded stretches), end-range strength, joint-specific mobility, gymnastics control |
| **Key Adaptations** | Injury resistance, movement economy, skill transfer |
| **Ceiling Constraint** | Time investment + nervous control |
| **Apex Benchmarks** | Full depth squat (heels down, neutral spine), arms overhead (no rib flare), hip IR 35°+, ASLR 80°+  |

**RULE:** Passive flexibility = weakness. Controlled range = lethality.

---

### STAT 5: STABILITY, BALANCE & COORDINATION — Control

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Total body control, zero energy leaks |
| **Training Style** | Rings, unstable implements, single-limb work, anti-rotational drills |
| **Key Adaptations** | Proprioception, motor learning speed, injury resilience |
| **Apex Benchmarks** | Single-leg balance (eyes closed) 30+s, L-sit 30-60s, Front plank 2+ min, Pistol squat 10/leg, Ring support 30s |

---

### STAT 6: BODY COMPOSITION — Power-to-Weight Ratio

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Maximum strength with minimal dead weight |
| **Strategy** | Cycled hypertrophy → consolidation, precision nutrition, strategic fat loss |
| **Ceiling Constraint** | Hormones, sleep, caloric availability |
| **Apex Range** | BF% 8-12% sustainable, Waist-to-height <0.45, Functional (not bulky) muscle mass |

**RULE:** Every unnecessary kg taxes speed, joints, endurance, and recovery.

---

### STAT 7: RECOVERY — THE HIDDEN STAT (REAL LIMITER)

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Target** | Sustain extreme output long-term |
| **Tools** | Sleep optimization (non-negotiable), parasympathetic training (breathwork), soft tissue care, deload cycles |
| **Apex Benchmarks** | Sleep 7.5-9h consistent, HR recovery post-effort ≥30bpm in 1min, DOMS <48h, 5-6 quality sessions/week |

**HARD TRUTH: Recovery capacity is the REAL limiter of max stats. Not training capacity.**

## THE TRADE-OFF MATRIX & ELITE RESOLUTION STRATEGY

| Conflicting Stats | The Conflict | Elite Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| Strength ↔ Endurance | Muscle mass vs efficiency | Rotate in blocks. Maintain MED on non-priority. |
| Speed ↔ Max Mass | Inertia | Never bulk. Power-to-weight over absolute mass. |
| Flexibility ↔ Stability | Requires balance | Active strength through range, not passive flexibility. |
| Peak Performance ↔ Longevity | Stress accumulation | Built-in deloads, conservative weekly structure. |

**Elite Strategy:**
```
1. Rotate EMPHASIS in blocks (not complete elimination)
2. Maintain MINIMUM EFFECTIVE DOSE for all non-priority stats
3. Peak SELECTIVELY at planned times
4. Never let any stat fall below functional floor
```
