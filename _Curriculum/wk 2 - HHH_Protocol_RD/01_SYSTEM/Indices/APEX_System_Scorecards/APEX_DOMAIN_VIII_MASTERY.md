## 2. BREAKING PLATEAUS (PERIODIZATION)
A plateau is not a failure; it is an adaptation.
*   **The Cause:** Your body has solved the puzzle you gave it.
*   **The Solution:** Change the puzzle.
    *   **Linear Periodization:** Good for beginners (Add weight every week).
    *   **Undulating Periodization:** Apex Standard (Change stress daily: Heavy / Fast / Volume).
    *   **Block Periodization:** Phase-specific focus (4 weeks Strength -> 4 weeks Power).
