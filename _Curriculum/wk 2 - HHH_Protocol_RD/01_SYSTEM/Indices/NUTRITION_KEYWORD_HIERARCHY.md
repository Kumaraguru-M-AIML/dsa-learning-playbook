## ⚡ LEVEL 3: THE SYSTEM SPARK PLUGS (MICRO)

### **[4.0] VITAMINS: Water-Soluble vs. Fat-Soluble**
*   **Water-Soluble Vitamins**: Consistent intake required (excreted easily).
    *   **B-Complex Suite**: B1, B2, B3, B5, B6, B7, B9, B12 (Energy & neuro-speed).
    *   **Vitamin C**: Immunity and tissue/collagen repair.
*   **Fat-Soluble Vitamins**: Stored in fatty tissues.
    *   **Vitamins A, D, E, K**: Essential for vision, bone density, and hormonal health.

### **[5.0] MINERALS: Electrolytes vs. Trace Minerals**
*   **Electrolytes**: Electrical balance and nerve-muscle signaling.
    *   **Sodium, Potassium, Chloride, Magnesium** (The 4:1 Ratio logic).
*   **Structural Minerals**: **Calcium, Silicon, Bromine** (Bones and teeth).
*   **Trace Minerals**: 
    *   **Established**: Iron (O2), Zinc (T), Iodine (Thyroid), Selenium.
    *   **Emerging**: Molybdenum, Chromium, Boron, Vanadium, Nickel.
