## 📘 OVERVIEW

This document provides a **systems engineering view** of human transformation. Just as a computer has Hardware, BIOS, OS, and Applications, the human body operates on a 10-layer stack. Understanding this architecture is critical because:

1. **Dependencies Matter**: You cannot run Applications without a functioning OS.
2. **Order of Operations**: Fixing Layer 3 (OS) before Layer 6 (Apps) saves years of wasted effort.
3. **System Debugging**: When progress stalls, you can identify which layer is failing.

**Core Principle**: "The HHH Protocol is not about adding more knowledge. It is about upgrading the system that processes knowledge."
