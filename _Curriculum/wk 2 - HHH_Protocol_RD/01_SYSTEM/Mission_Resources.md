# HHH PROTOCOL: RESOURCE & REQUIREMENT MAP
*The complete inventory for the journey from civilian to apex.*

## 🧬 DIMENSION 1 & 2: PHYSICAL FRAME & AVATAR
### Essential (Scarcity)
- **Resistance Source**: Bodyweight, gravity, or heavy stones/sandbags.
- **Gravity Hub**: A pull-up bar, tree branch, or sturdy ledge.
- **Structural Tools**: A wall (for posture/postural realignment) and the floor (for stretches/holds).
- **Tongue Posture (Mewing)**: $0 cost, requires 100% awareness.

### Optimized (Lavish)
- **Hormonal Data**: Regular blood panels (GH, IGF-1, Testosterone, Cortisol).
- **Skeletal Tools**: Inversion table (for decompression), high-quality weighted vests.
- **Soft Tissue**: High-end skincare (Retinoids, Vitamin C, specialized moisturizers).

## ⚔️ DIMENSION 3: PHYSICAL SUPREMACY (Combat & Muscle)
### Essential (Scarcity)
- **Open Space**: A 6x6 foot area for burpees, sprints, and shadowboxing.
- **External Load**: A backpack filled with books or rocks (for weighted walk/rucking).
- **Combat Source**: A partner or a heavy bag (even a tree wrapped in carpet).

### Optimized (Lavish)
- **The Modern Gym**: Squat racks, cable machines, and isolation equipment.
- **Combat training**: Formal coaching in BJJ, Muay Thai, or Boxing.
- **Recovery Gear**: Percussion massagers (Theragun), sauna/cold plunge access.

## 🧠 DIMENSION 4: NEURO-DOMINANCE (Cognition)
### Essential (Scarcity)
- **Deep Work Zone**: A dedicated desk or spot with zero distractions.
- **Information Filter**: Ad-blockers, app-limiters (to protect dopamine).
- **Journal**: Paper and pen (for strategy and emotional audits).
- **The Library**: Your existing 91 extraction files (already in `E:\HHH_Protocol_RD\01_Bio_Foundation\Extracted_Mastery`).

### Optimized (Lavish)
- **Nootropics**: Caffeine (L-Theanine stack), Magnesium Threonate, Lion's Mane.
- **Tech**: Noise-canceling headphones (Sony/Bose) and high-performance workstation.
- **Guided Pressure**: High-stakes environments (investing, public speaking, leadership roles).

## 🔋 DIMENSION 8: RECOVERY & FUEL
### Essential (Scarcity)
- **Sleep Hygiene**: Blackout curtains (or a cloth over eyes) and a cold room.
- **The Staples**: Eggs, organ meats (liver), seasonal vegetables, rice/potatoes, salt, water.
- **Sunlight**: 10-20 minutes of early morning sun exposure.

### Optimized (Lavish)
- **Precision Nutrition**: Grass-fed beef, wild-caught fish, specialized supplements (Vitamin D3/K2, Omega-3).
- **Sleep Tech**: Specialized mattresses (standardizing temp) and sleep trackers (Oura, Whoop).
