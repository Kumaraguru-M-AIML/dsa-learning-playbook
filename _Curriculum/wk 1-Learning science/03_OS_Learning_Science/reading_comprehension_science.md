# Domain Science: Reading Comprehension

> **Status**: 🚀 Frontier Document (New R&D)
> **Focus**: The cognitive architecture of extracting meaning from text
> **Theoretical Basis**: Simple View of Reading (SVR), Construction-Integration Model (Kintsch)
> **Neuroscience Focus**: Visual Word Form Area (VWFA), Deep Reading vs. Skimming Circuits

---

## 1. THE FOUNDATION: SIMPLE VIEW OF READING (SVR)

### 🔬 Scientific Mechanism
**The Equation**: R = D × LC
*   **Decoding (D)**: The ability to translate print into speech. (Zero for non-readers).
*   **Language Comprehension (LC)**: The ability to understand spoken language.
*   **Implication**: If D=0 (cannot read words), Reading is 0. If LC=0 (can read words but doesn't know what they mean), Reading is 0.

### 🔑 Key Variables
*   **The Matthew Effect**: "The rich get richer." Strong decoders read more -> learn more vocab -> have higher LC -> become better readers. Weak decoders read less -> lose vocab -> fall further behind.

---

## 2. THE ENGINE: CONSTRUCTION-INTEGRATION (CI) MODEL

### 🔬 Scientific Mechanism
**Walter Kintsch's Model**: How we build a mental movie from black squiggles.
1.  **Phase 1: Construction (The Dumb Activation)**
    *   The brain activates ALL meanings of a word. When you read "The man approached the bank," your brain activates (a) *Financial Bank* AND (b) *River Bank*.
2.  **Phase 2: Integration (The Smart Pruning)**
    *   Context kicks in. If the next sentence is "He fished," the brain aggressively inhibits the "Financial" node and strengthens the "River" node.
3.  **Phase 3: The Situation Model**
    *   The final product is not a string of words, but a non-verbal mental simulation of the event.

### 🚧 Edge Cases & Pitfalls
*   **Integration Failure**: Poor readers often get stuck in Phase 1. They keep inconsistent meanings active or fail to build a coherent Situation Model, ending up with "fragmented" understanding.

## 3. NEUROSCIENCE: DEEP READING VS. SCROLLING

### 🧠 The Neural Circuitry
*   **Deep Reading (Bi-literate Brain)**: Requires milliseconds of "cognitive patience."
    *   **Pathway**: VWFA (See) -> Wernicke's (Meaning) -> Prefrontal/Parietal (Analysis/Empathy).
    *   **Outcome**: Builds "Crystalized Knowledge" and empathy.
*   **Skimming (Digital Brain)**: A "F-pattern" scan.
    *   **Neuroplasticity**: The brain adapts to the medium. Chronic scrolling literally *atrophies* the deep reading circuits. We are losing the ability to read complex arguments because our brains are rewiring for "information spotting."

### 🔬 Maryanne Wolf's Warning
*   **"Proust and the Squid"**: We were not born to read. It is a cultural invention that hijacked visual object recognition circuits. If we stop training deep reading, we revert to a simpler, more reactive cognitive state.

## 4. PROTOCOLS & TECHNIQUES

### 🏋️ Technique 1: SQ3R (Systematic Architecture)
*   **Protocol**:
    1.  **Survey**: Scan headers/images (Build the scaffolding).
    2.  **Question**: Turn headers into questions (Open the dopamine loops).
    3.  **Read**: Find the answers (Close the loops).
    4.  **Recite**: Say the answer out loud (Testing Effect).
    5.  **Review**: Synthesize.
*   **Mechanism**: Pre-activates the "Situation Model" (step 1-2) so the "Integration" phase (step 3) is faster and more robust.

### 🏋️ Technique 2: Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP)
*   **Protocol**: Spritzing words one at a time at 500wpm.
*   **Verdict**: Good for **Skimming** (locating info), bad for **Comprehension** (building Situation Models). The brain needs the "parafoveal preview" (seeing the next word) and "regressions" (looking back) to handle the Construction-Integration process.

### 🏋️ Technique 3: Marginalia (Active Dialogue)
*   **Protocol**: Never read without a pen. Argue with the author in the margins.
*   **Mechanism**: Forces "Elaborative Encoding." You are not just downloading the text; you are linking it to your existing neural network.
