## 🎓 APPLICATION PROTOCOL: "THE SPEED MASTER"

### Step 1: The Pre-Read (3 min)
*   Scan the table of contents and headings. 
*   *Purpose*: Create the **Scaffolding** for the info to land on.

### Step 2: The Fast-Read (20 min)
*   Use a pacer (finger or pen). No regressions (No going back).
*   *Purpose*: High-speed data ingestion.

### Step 3: The Reconstruction (10 min)
*   Close the book. **Active Recall** every major node.
*   Draw a **Mind Map** (Krasnic link) from memory.

### Step 4: The Encoding (15 min)
*   Take the nodes from Step 3 and place them in a permanent **Memory Palace**.
