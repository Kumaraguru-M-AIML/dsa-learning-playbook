# Sleep Consolidation

## The Concept (Atomic Idea)
Consolidation—the process where fragile, short-term memories are integrated into robust, long-term neural networks—happens primarily during sleep, not during the act of studying itself.

---

## Elaboration / Examples
Cramming is biologically inefficient because it deprives the brain of the necessary offline processing time (specifically SWS - Slow Wave Sleep and REM).
- You "encode" while awake and focused.
- You "consolidate" and build actual permanent skill while asleep.
- Therefore, protecting sleep hygiene is a non-negotiable prerequisite for high-level mastery.
