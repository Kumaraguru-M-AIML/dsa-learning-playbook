# Dopamine approach behavior

## The Concept (Atomic Idea)
Dopamine is not primarily a "reward" molecule; it is a molecule of *motivation and drive*. It dictates "approach behavior"—the willingness to expend effort and close the gap between your current state and a desired goal.

---

## Elaboration / Examples
If you find yourself procrastinating on a learning task, it is often a dopamine issue (lack of drive), not a lack of innate intelligence. 
- Structuring learning with micro-wins and clear, immediate feedback establishes a healthy dopamine cycle.
- **Triadic Dominance (TDS):** Dopamine heavily influences the "Approach vs Avoidance" state.
