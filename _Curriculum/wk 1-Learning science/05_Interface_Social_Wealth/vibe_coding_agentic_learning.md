## 1. THE "VIBE CODING" PHENOMENON

### 🔬 The Concept
Traditional coding (Syntax-Heavy) is a **Black Box** for most. **Vibe Coding** is a high-level **White Box** approach where the "Vibe" (Intellectual Intent + Architecture) is controlled by the human, while the "Syntax" is generated by the AI (LLM).

### Core Principles
1.  **Architecture over Syntax**: The master learns *how* the app should work, not *how* to write every loop.
2.  **Iteration Loops**: Rapid prompting and debugging (Systematic Error Injection).
3.  **The Agentic Loop**: Using AI agents (like me) to verify and execute, while the human acts as the orchestrator (Regulatory System).

## 2. THE AI-LEARNING PROTOCOL

### Stage 1: Intent Deconstruction
*   Break the goal into "Minimum Viable Logic" steps.
*   *Protocol*: Use the **Feynman Technique** to explain the "Vibe" of your project to the AI.

### Stage 2: Prompt Engineering as Cognitive Scaffolding
*   Treat the AI as an "External Prefrontal Cortex."
*   *Technique*: **Chain of Thought (CoT)** prompting to mirror your internal logical structure.

### Stage 3: The Debugging Metacognition
*   When the AI fails, you are in the **Grey Box Phase**.
*   *Strategy*: Audit the "Vibe" (Intent) vs. the "Code" (Execution). Where is the misalignment?
