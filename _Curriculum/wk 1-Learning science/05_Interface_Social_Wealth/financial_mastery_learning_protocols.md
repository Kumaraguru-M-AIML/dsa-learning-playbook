*   **The Skill**: English + Specific Technical Skill (Design/Web).
*   **RSA Method**: **Parsons Problems** for coding; **Copy-Work** for design/copywriting.
*   **Mastery Goal**: White-boxing the client's problem so you can solve it faster than competitors.

### B. The "Algorithm" Protocol (Content/YouTube)
*   **The Skill**: Audience Psychology + Retention.
*   **RSA Method**: **Video Analysis (Social Learning)**. Watch top creators with the sound OFF to see pacing/visuals.
*   **Mastery Goal**: Understanding the **Reward Prediction Error** of the audience.

### C. The "Passive" Protocol (Digital Products)
*   **The Skill**: Systems Thinking + Scalability.
*   **RSA Method**: **Combinatorial Creativity**. Mixing existing templates (Notion/Canva) into new solutions.
*   **Mastery Goal**: Building "Rules-Based Systems" that operate without you.
