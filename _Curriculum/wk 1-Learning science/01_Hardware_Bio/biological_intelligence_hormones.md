## 2. THE ENDOCRINE MASTER CYCLE

### 🌞 The Cortisol Wake-up Response (CWR)
*   First 90-120 mins of waking: Peak focus and alertness.
*   *Protocol*: **Eat the Frog**. Solve your most avoided problem here.

### 🌓 The Post-Prandial Dip (Dopamine Trough)
*   2:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Natural slump.
*   *Protocol*: Passive learning (Podcasts, Videos) or "Zoning Out" (DMN activation).

### 🌑 The Consolidation Abyss (Sleep)
*   Hormonal reset. Sifting of short-term into long-term memory.
*   *Requirement*: 7-9 hours. NO HACK can replace this.
