# Physical & Lifestyle Variables: Deep Dive

> **Status**: Deepened R&D Reference Document
> **Focus**: Physiological factors regulating cognitive performance.
> **Goal**: To treat the body as the hardware platform for the software of learning.

---

## 1. STRESS-RECOVERY BALANCE (HRV)

### 🔬 What Is It?
**Heart Rate Variability (HRV)** is the variance in time between the beats of your heart. It is the most accurate non-invasive metric of the Autonomic Nervous System (ANS).
*   **High HRV**: Parasympathetic dominance (Rest & Digest). High cognitive readiness.
*   **Low HRV**: Sympathetic dominance (Fight or Flight). High stress, low readiness.

### 🎓 Application
*   **The "Traffic Light" System**: Measure HRV upon waking.
    *   **Green (High)**: Go for maximum cognitive load (New material, complex problems).
    *   **Red (Low)**: System is fried. Focus on recovery, shallow work, or review. *Do not attempt deep learning.*

## 2. INTEROCEPTION (The Sixth Sense)

### 🔬 What Is It?
The sense of the internal state of the body (hunger, heartbeat, breath, bladder).
*   **The Insula**: The brain region that maps these sensations.
*   **The Link**: High interoceptive awareness correlates with high **Emotional Regulation**. If you can "feel" the stress rising (racing heart), you can regulate it *before* it hijacks your PFC.

### 🎓 Application
*   **"HALT" Protocol**: Before studying, check:
    *   **H**ungry? (Glucose crash imminent).
    *   **A**ngry? (Amygdala hijacking).
    *   **L**onely? (Social pain = Physical pain).
    *   **T**ired? (Adenosine buildup).

## 3. ULTRADIAN RHYTHMS (The 90-Minute Wave)

### 🔬 What Is It?
While Circadian rhythms are 24 hours, **Ultradian** rhythms are shorter cycles throughout the day.
*   **BRAC (Basic Rest-Activity Cycle)**: The brain can sustain high-frequency beta-wave focus for approximately **90 minutes**.
*   **The Valley**: After 90 minutes, the brain accumulated metabolic waste and drops into a "healing" trough (20 mins).

### 🎓 Application
*   **The Macro-Pomodoro**: Instead of 25/5, try **90/20**.
*   **Respect the Dip**: When you hit the wall at 90 mins, *stop*. Pushing through just produces "garbage code" (low-quality encoding).

---

## 4. SLEEP ARCHITECTURE (The Cleaning Crew)
*   **SWS (Deep Sleep)**: Physical repair + Declarative Memory (Facts). First half of night.
*   **REM (Dream Sleep)**: Emotional repair + Procedural Memory (Skills) + Creativity. Second half of night.
*   **Rule**: Cutting sleep from 8h to 6h cuts the *last 2 hours*, which is where 80% of REM happens. You act "brain damaged" regarding emotional IQ and creativity.

---

## 5. EXERCISE (BDNF)
*   **Mechanism**: Aerobic exercise releases **BDNF** (Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor).
*   **Timing**: 20 minutes of moderate cardio *immediately before* specific learning tasks creates the most fertile neuroplastic window.

---

## 6. NUTRITION (Fuel)
*   **Glucose**: The brain needs steady fuel. Complex carbs > Sugar spikes.
*   **Hydration**: 2% dehydration = 10% cognitive drop.
*   **Omega-3s**: Myelin sheath integrity (Speed of thought).
