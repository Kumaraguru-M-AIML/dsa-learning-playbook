## 4. Operational Protocols

### 🟢 Normal Operation
1.  Navigate to your workspace (e.g., `E:\wk 1-Learning science`).
2.  Run `python summon_sol.py`.
3.  Issue command: `think "My Goal"`.

### 🟡 Diagnostic Mode (If acting strange)
1.  Run `status` in the CLI. 
    *   Check **Memory Core** (Must be ONLINE).
    *   Check **Scaffolding Level** (Is it too high/low?).

### 🔴 Emergency Reset (If crashed/stuck)
1.  Delete the local `.brain` folder in your workspace (The Global Brain is safe in `C:\...`).
2.  Run `python summon_sol.py` to re-initialize a fresh local cortex.
3.  Your **User Stats** are preserved in the Global Cortex.
