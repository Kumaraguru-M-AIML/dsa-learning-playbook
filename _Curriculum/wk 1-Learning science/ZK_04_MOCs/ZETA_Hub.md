# Welcome to the ZETA System (Zettelkasten Environment for Thinking & Assembly)

The ZETA System is your engine for turning information consumption into robust, interconnected knowledge. It acts as the "Processing Layer" between your raw Source materials (Deep Dives, Articles, Books) and your Output/Mastery.

## The ZETA Workflow (The 3 Stages of Knowledge)

1. **[[ZK_01_Inbox/Index|Capture (Fleeting Notes)]]**: Quick thoughts, immediate ideas during the day.
2. **[[ZK_02_Literature/Index|Synthesize (Literature Notes)]]**: Highlighting, summarizing, and extracting key insights from your Deep Dive documents.
3. **[[ZK_03_Permanent/Index|Connect (Permanent Notes)]]**: The core of the system. Atomic, self-contained ideas linked together to form a network of thought.

## How to use this system with your Deep Dives:
1. When doing your Daily Execution (e.g., Reading `neuroscience_deep_dive.md`), treat the deep dive as a "Source".
2. Extract the best insights and create **Permanent Notes** in the `ZK_03_Permanent` folder.
3. Link the Permanent Note back to the deep dive.
4. Link the new Permanent Note to other existing ideas and MOCs.

> "A well-maintained Zettelkasten is a conversation partner, not a storage unit."
