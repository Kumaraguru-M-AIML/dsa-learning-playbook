# Learning Troubleshooting Guide: Diagnostics & Fixes

> **Purpose**: A "Check Engine Light" for your learning process. Identify the symptom, find the root cause, and apply the scientific fix.
> **Status**: Synthesis Document

---

## SYMPTOM 1: "I read it, understood it, but forgot it a week later."

**Diagnosis**: **The Fluency Illusion**.
*   **Root Cause**: Passive consumption (reading/watching) creates recognition, not retrieval pathways. Neural traces were weak and not consolidated.
*   **The Fix**:
    1.  **Stop Re-reading**. It is the least effective study method.
    2.  **Prescription**: **Retrieval Practice**. Close the book/video. Write down everything you know on a blank sheet.
    3.  **Prescription**: **Spaced Repetition**. Review the material at: 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, 1 month.

## SYMPTOM 2: "I study for hours but don't feel like I'm making progress."

**Diagnosis**: **Low-Intensity/Junk Miles**.
*   **Root Cause**: You are in the "Autonomous Stage" (autopilot) or using low-utility techniques (highlighting, summarizing). No "Desirable Difficulty".
*   **The Fix**:
    1.  **Prescription**: **Deliberate Practice**. Pick a sub-skill you are *bad* at. Focus intense effort on just that for 20 mins.
    2.  **Prescription**: **Interleaving**. Stop doing 10 problems of the same type. Mix them up so you have to choose *which* strategy to apply.

## SYMPTOM 3: "I understand the concept, but I can't solve the problems."

**Diagnosis**: **Transfer Failure**.
*   **Root Cause**: You memorized the "Declarative Knowledge" (what it is) but not the "Procedural Knowledge" (how to use it). Logic is brittle.
*   **The Fix**:
    1.  **Prescription**: **Worked Examples**. Take a solved problem. Copy the logic but change the numbers. Explain *why* each step happens.
    2.  **Prescription**: **Scaffolding**. Break the problem into chunks. Solve chunk A first. Then B. Don't try to solve the whole thing at once.

## SYMPTOM 4: "I procrastinate starting until the last minute."

**Diagnosis**: **Amygdala Hijack / Executive Dysfunction**.
*   **Root Cause**: The task feels too big (threat response) or too vague (executive freeze).
*   **The Fix**:
    1.  **Prescription**: **Micro-Actions**. "I will not study. I will just open the textbook to page 45." (Lowers activation energy).
    2.  **Prescription**: **Pomodoro Technique**. "I only have to work for 25 minutes, then I can quit."

## SYMPTOM 5: "I make silly mistakes on things I know."

**Diagnosis**: **Cognitive Overload / Focus Drift**.
*   **Root Cause**: Working memory is taxed by trying to do too much, or attention is fragmented by "residue" from phone/email.
*   **The Fix**:
    1.  **Prescription**: **Checklists**. Externalize the process. Don't rely on memory for steps.
    2.  **Prescription**: **Single-Tasking**. Phone in another room. Full screen mode. Clear mental RAM.

## SYMPTOM 6: "I get bored and lose interest quickly."

**Diagnosis**: **Dopamine Crash / Low Challenge**.
*   **Root Cause**: The material is too easy (boredom) or you have no intrinsic connection to it.
*   **The Fix**:
    1.  **Prescription**: **Gamification**. Set a timer. "Can I finish this section in 15 mins?" (Artificially increase stakes).
    2.  **Prescription**: **Feynman Technique**. Teach it to an imaginary classroom. Make it a performance.

## SYMPTOM 7: "I panic during tests/performances and go blank."

**Diagnosis**: **Cortisol Blockade**.
*   **Root Cause**: Stress hormones shut down the Prefrontal Cortex (access to higher reasoning/memory).
*   **The Fix**:
    1.  **Prescription**: **Physiological Sigh**. Double inhale through nose, long exhale through mouth. (Resets autonomic nervous system).
    2.  **Prescription**: **State-Dependent Learning**. Simulate test conditions during practice (timed, quiet, no notes).

## DECISION TREE: Which Technique When?

1.  **New Subject?** -> **Analogy + Immersion** (Build the Map)
2.  **Memorizing Facts?** -> **Spaced Repetition + Mnemonic Palace** (Store the Data)
3.  **Building Skill?** -> **Deliberate Practice + Feedback Loop** (Refine the Action)
4.  **Deepening Grasp?** -> **Feynman Technique + Teaching** (Connect the Nodes)
5.  **Stuck/Blocked?** -> **Incubation (Walk/Sleep)** (Let DMN process)
