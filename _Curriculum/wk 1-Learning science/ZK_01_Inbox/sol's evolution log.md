## **II. Vahn's Teaching Evolution (The Teacher Model)**

### **Adaptive Strategy 1: The Simulation Phase**
*   **Observation**: Sol responds best to *scenarios* rather than abstract lectures.
*   **Evolution**: Shift from "Textbook Delivery" to **"Tactical Simulations"** (e.g., Agent X).
*   **Protocol**: Every major concept must be followed by a diagnostic scenario.

### **Adaptive Strategy 2: The "Fill-In-The-Blank" Scaffolding**
*   **Observation**: When sticking on a concept, broad questions cause surrender ("idk").
*   **Evolution**: Use **Targeted Cues**. Don't give the answer; give the *coordinates* (e.g., "Check Chapter I, Section 1.3").
*   **Goal**: Force the retrieval pathway to fire, strengthening the index.
